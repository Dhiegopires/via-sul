## WHAT TO PRODUCE

**Part 1: Sitemap**
Define every page the site needs. For each page:
- Proposed URL (clean, semantic, lowercase, hyphens, no accents)
- Page title
- Page goal: what this page exists to do (rank / convert / inform / support)
- Primary keyword: the one keyword this page targets (must come from the keyword map)
- Primary CTA: the one action the visitor should take
- Priority: high / medium / low for development order

Only include pages that have a clear goal and a keyword to rank for. Do not pad the sitemap. A tight sitemap of 6 real pages beats a bloated sitemap of 15 hollow ones.

**Exception:** if any page on the site collects personal data (a lead form, WhatsApp click, booking widget), the sitemap must include a `/politica-de-privacidade/` page even though it targets no keyword and drives no conversion — it exists to satisfy LGPD, not to rank. Mark its goal as "compliance," priority low for development order, but present at launch, not an afterthought.

**Part 2: Intent mapping**
For the 5 most important pages (or all pages if the sitemap has fewer than 5 pages in total), define:
- The search intent this page satisfies (informational / commercial / transactional)
- The persona who lands on this page and their mindset when they arrive
- The funnel stage: top / middle / bottom
- The sections this page must contain to satisfy intent and convert (list only, no copy yet)
- The internal links this page should send and receive

**Part 3: Internal linking structure**
Define the link architecture. Which pages link to which, with what anchor text. Show the hierarchy clearly: which pages are hubs, which are leaves.

**Part 4: CTA logic summary**
One table showing: page → primary CTA → secondary CTA → what happens after the CTA (where the user goes).

## OUTPUT FORMAT

Gere um arquivo `.md` salvo como `sitemap-[cliente].md` na pasta do projeto. Seções em ordem:

1. **Cabeçalho**: nome do cliente, data, total de páginas, prioridades por nível
2. **Sitemap completo**: árvore markdown de todas as páginas — URL limpa, objetivo da página, keyword primária
3. **Intent mapping das 5 páginas principais**: sub-seção por página — objetivo de conversão, persona, keyword primária, CTAs previstos, prioridade
4. **Arquitetura de links internos**: tabela `Página Origem | Página Destino | Anchor Text`
5. **Lógica de CTAs**: tabela `Página | CTA Principal | Destino | CTA Secundário`

Para dados ausentes: `> ⚠️ [DATA NEEDED: descreva o que falta]`

## TONE RULES

- Every page in the sitemap must have a stated reason to exist
- Every keyword must come from the keyword map provided. Do not invent keywords.
- Every CTA must be specific: "Book a 15-min call" not "Contact us"
- Flag any assumption you make about the site structure explicitly
- No AI language anywhere in the document

## CRITICAL AI GUARDRAILS (HALLUCINATION-PROOFING)

To prevent AI hallucinations and generic "slop" output, you must strictly follow these rules:
1. **No Keyword Inventions**: Do not map pages to keywords that are not present in the provided Keyword Map input. If a page does not have an approved keyword target, flag it with `[KEYWORD NEEDED]`.
2. **No Dummy/Placeholder URLs**: All URLs must follow the lowercase-hyphen structure. Do not use generic placeholders like `/temp-page/` or `/placeholder/`.
3. **No AI Copy Patterns**:
   - Do not use em dashes (`—`) in copy.
   - Do not use emojis in written body text or headlines.
   - Do not use SaaS clichés ("Not only X, but Y", "Transform your business").
