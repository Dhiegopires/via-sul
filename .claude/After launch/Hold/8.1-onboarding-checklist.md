# Monthly Plan Onboarding: First Month Checklist

> Run this checklist in the first week after launch. Do not generate the first monthly report until every item is checked.
> This file is for you. Not for the AI.
> See "GA4 + Hotjar: where to find things and what they mean" in `README.md` for exactly where each of these lives in the GA4/Hotjar interface — bounce rate and conversion rate are not visible by default in GA4.

---

## Access setup

### Google Analytics (both tiers)

GA4 should already be live — it's installed at launch in `6-final-checklist/` for every project, regardless of retainer. This section confirms VellumWire's own access and pulls the existing baseline, it does not set up GA4 from scratch unless Step 6 was skipped (e.g. the client launched before joining the monthly plan with another vendor).

- [ ] GA4 property confirmed live on the site (check Step 6 records, or verify directly if this client launched elsewhere)
- [ ] Conversion events are configured and firing:
  - [ ] Form submission event
  - [ ] Confirmed-lead event (`generate_lead` or equivalent) — for any CTA that hands off to an external tool (Calendly, Typeform, WhatsApp), verify this fires on actual completion, not on the outbound click. See `../../Full-website/5-development/README.md` Conversion Tracking section if it doesn't.
  - [ ] CTA click event (`cta_click` or equivalent) — a separate, lower-tier signal kept for funnel diagnosis. Do not treat this as the lead count; a page can show high clicks and zero confirmed leads, which points at the external tool or the offer, not at page structure.
  - [ ] Phone number click (if applicable)
- [ ] VellumWire has Viewer access to the GA4 property (email: [your Google account])
- [ ] Baseline confirmed: pull the session count and conversion rate from the launch-week record (Step 6) if available; otherwise note the earliest available week as the baseline

If GA4 is missing entirely (this client did not go through Step 6 with VellumWire): install it now before the monthly plan begins. This is not optional — without data, there is no report.

### Hotjar (Build only)

- [ ] Client has Hotjar installed on the new site
- [ ] Heatmaps recording on:
  - [ ] Homepage
  - [ ] Primary service or product page
  - [ ] Contact or lead capture page
- [ ] Scroll depth tracking confirmed active
- [ ] VellumWire has access to Hotjar account (invite to: [your email])
- [ ] Baseline recording started: at least 100 sessions before reading heatmaps

If the client does not have Hotjar: recommend they start the free plan. Do not absorb the Hotjar subscription — it stays on the client's account.

## Onboarding note to send the client

Send this after access is confirmed and before the first report:

> Hi [Name],
>
> Everything is set up on our end. Here's what to expect every month:
>
> On [day of month], you'll receive a 1-page report showing traffic, lead sources, and conversion activity for the previous month. [Build: You'll also get one specific recommendation based on what the data shows, and a summary of what we built or tested that month.]
>
> If you need any text, image, or contact information updated on the site during the month, send it over and we'll handle it within [2 business days].
>
> [Build: Our monthly call is scheduled for [day/time]. We'll use it to align on the next month's focus.]
>
> Anything outside the plan scope — new pages, new sections, new functionality — I'll scope and quote before starting. No surprises.
>
